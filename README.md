# 📊 Product Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📖 Overview

This project analyzes product sales data using Power BI to uncover key business insights related to revenue generation, profitability, regional performance, and seasonal trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable intelligence that supports strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard was developed to answer the following questions:

- 💰 Which products generate the highest revenue and profit?
- 🌍 Which regions contribute most to overall sales?
- 🍂 How do seasonal patterns influence business performance?
- 📈 What factors drive profitability?
- 🚀 What recommendations can improve business growth and financial performance?

---

## 📋 Dashboard Pages

### 🏆 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance through key metrics:

- 💵 Total Revenue
- 📈 Total Profit
- 📊 Profit Margin
- 📦 Total Units Sold

This page enables stakeholders to quickly evaluate overall business performance.

![Executive Overview](screenshots/executive_overview.png)

---

### 💰 2. Product Revenue Analysis

Analyzes product-level performance to identify top revenue and profit contributors.

#### 🔍 Key Findings

- 🥇 Paseo generated the highest revenue and profit.
- 🥈 VTT and Velo were strong secondary performers.
- 📉 Carretera generated the lowest revenue.
- 📊 Profit margins varied significantly across products.

#### 🎯 Business Value

Helps identify products that should receive greater investment, marketing focus, and inventory support.

![Product Revenue Analysis](screenshots/product_revenue_analysis.png)

---

### 🌍 3. Regional and Seasonal Analysis

Examines sales performance across countries and seasons.

#### 🔍 Key Findings

##### 🌎 Top Performing Markets

- 🇩🇪 Germany
- 🇫🇷 France
- 🇨🇦 Canada
- 🇺🇸 United States

##### 🍁 Seasonal Performance

- 🥇 Autumn recorded the highest sales.
- 🥈 Winter was the second strongest season.
- 📉 Spring recorded the lowest sales.

#### 🎯 Business Value

Supports market prioritization, inventory planning, and seasonal marketing strategies.

![Regional and Seasonal Analysis](screenshots/regional_seasonal_analysis.png)

---

### 📈 4. Profitability Analysis

Investigates the factors influencing profit generation.

#### 🔍 Key Findings

- 💰 Total profit exceeded **$16.8 million**.
- 🏆 Paseo generated the highest overall profit.
- 📦 Higher sales volumes generally resulted in higher profits.
- 📊 Profit trends fluctuated throughout the year, suggesting seasonal influences.

#### 🎯 Business Value

Supports pricing decisions, product optimization, and financial planning.

![Profitability Analysis](screenshots/profitability_analysis.png)

---

## 💡 Key Recommendations

### 🚀 Revenue Growth

- Continue investing in high-performing products such as Paseo.
- Expand distribution and marketing efforts in top-performing regions.

### 📈 Profitability Improvement

- Review pricing strategies for mid-performing products.
- Focus on improving profit margins alongside revenue growth.

### 📅 Seasonal Planning

- Increase promotional activities before peak seasons.
- Introduce targeted campaigns during lower-performing periods.

### 🌍 Market Expansion

- Investigate lower-performing markets for growth opportunities.
- Develop localized marketing strategies to increase market penetration.
